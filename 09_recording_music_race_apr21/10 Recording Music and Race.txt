=== SLIDE 6 ===
TITLE: Progress in recording technology

CONTENT:
1877 Edison invents tin foil on cylinder phonograph; recordings lasted a few plays and could not be preserved
1886 Edison returns to phonograph: permanent wax cylinders
1888 Entertainment recording begins
1889 Columbia Phonograph Company sold to exhibitors in fairs, automatic machines (juke boxes)
late 1890s phonographs sold to public; no major performers recorded (not lucrative enough)
